Helpful Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

The best months are from March to May and September to November. The weather is pleasant during these times.

Getting a Visa

Most travelers need a visa. You can often apply online for an e-visa. It is a simple process that takes a few days.

What to Pack

Pack modest clothes for respect. Women should have a light scarf for visiting mosques. Always have comfortable shoes for walking.

Safety Note: Pakistan is safe for tourists. The government wants more visitors. They have special tourist police in popular areas. Use common sense like you would anywhere. The biggest surprise for most visitors is the hospitality of Pakistani people.
